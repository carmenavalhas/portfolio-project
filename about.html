About Me

Hello there. I am Carmen Navalhas. Front-end Developer and Graphic Designer, based in Portugal.

I've been developing my passion for graphic aesthetics and I have a thing for visual organisation. Working as a Front-end developer allows me to combine my graphic designer skills working on web code and tech side with my passion for visual arts. I think that creating and improving visual identities is one of the most beautiful ways to communicate.


"A good design can simplify a very complicated world."


Nowadays the user experience on Websites and Apps definitely is an involuntary chasing for simplicity and visual poetry. Isnt't it? On a personal level I am fond of all the forms of visual arts, design and cinema. Regarding to sports I love running which helps me to keep a healthy life balance.
I hope I can inspire you in some way. Thank you for visiting my website!
